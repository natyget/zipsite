Legal

Transparent, privacy-first practices.

ZipSite respects the talent community and the agencies we serve. Review our policies below.

Terms of Service

By using ZipSite, you agree to submit accurate information and respect the privacy of other talent. ZipSite may update these Terms to reflect product changes; we will notify you of major updates via email.

Acceptable use

  • Submit only assets you own or have rights to distribute.
  • Do not attempt to reverse engineer or resell ZipSite infrastructure.
  • Respect agency decisions and booking policies.

Subscriptions

Pro subscriptions renew monthly. Cancel anytime before renewal to avoid further charges. Pro features remain active through the end of the billing cycle.

Privacy Policy

We collect application data, portfolio media, and analytics solely to power the ZipSite platform. We do not sell personal data. Third-party processing is limited to infrastructure (hosting, email) and analytics providers under strict agreements.

Your rights

  • Request export or deletion of your profile at any time.
  • Access a log of AI edits performed on your bio and stats.
  • Opt out of marketing communications with a single click.

Data retention

We store talent data while accounts remain active. Archived profiles retain basic metadata for audit purposes, with imagery purged after 90 days.

Cookies & tracking

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and analytics cookies to understand product performance. Manage preferences in your account settings at any time.
