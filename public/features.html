Product overview

Every deliverable in sync.

ZipSite brings the precision of an editorial studio to AI automation. Each feature keeps the experience cohesive for scouts, bookers, and talent.

Product pillars

From intake to delivery, ZipSite handles the administrative lift so agents can focus on casting.

The Brain

Groq’s high-speed models transform raw bios into third-person profiles, ensure heights and measurements read correctly, and structure every detail.

The Storefront

A single responsive template renders thousands of portfolios. Typography, spacing, and imagery all stay consistent with agency expectations.

Asset control

Smart tagging, lazy loading, and skeleton states manage imagery elegantly, so boards feel cinematic while staying fast.

Analytics insight

See which portfolios land with agencies and surface demand by region, role, or recent bookings, all within a minimal dashboard.

Collaboration-ready

Send secure review links, add agency notes, and export PDF comp cards with custom metadata for your archive.

Scalable governance

Permissions, watermark logic, and pro upgrades stay centralized so your pipeline can grow without extra headcount.

Luxury feel. Lightning speed.

ZipSite inherits the high-gloss aesthetic of leading agencies but delivers through API-driven automation. The result: modern editorial feel without the production lag.

Editorial typography

Playfair Display headlines and Inter body copy mirror fashion publications. Large type, generous whitespace, and precise accent color guide attention.

Precision imagery

Full-bleed hero photography, grayscale hover transitions, and responsive grids echo the cinematic agency look.
