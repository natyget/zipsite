Process

Automation designed for bookers.

The ZipSite pipeline moves from talent intake to agency-ready deliverables in minutes. No spreadsheets, no email backlogs, just clean editorial output.

1. Capture

Talent apply through a multi-step flow purpose-built for agencies. Fields stay structured, and drag-and-drop uploads keep media organized.

Vitals & measurements

Height, size, and location inputs normalize automatically, ensuring consistent format across your roster.

Image discipline

Applicants tag each asset (Headshot, Beauty, Full Length) while the system enforces dimension and file-size guidance.

Intake highlights

  • • Drag & drop or direct upload
  • • Inline validation & hints
  • • Save progress tokens
  • • Accessible controls for every field

2. Curate

Groq-powered AI rewrites bios in the third person, enforces tonal consistency, and standardizes stats so every profile reads like a trusted comp.

Language refinement

Friendly prompts transform informal copy into polished editorial prose that fits your brand voice.

Structured data

Measurements, city names, and pronouns align to your style guide before they reach an agent.

Quality controls

Human review toggles let your directors approve edge cases, while automated checks catch duplicates and missing media.

3. Present

The storefront template powers both the free comp card PDF and the pro portfolio link. Deliverables stay print-minded and ready for booking.

PDF comp card

5.5 × 8.5 inch format with safe area, metadata, and watermark control. Ready for print or quick send.

Live pro site

Responsive layout, embedded reels, and contact CTAs. Subscribers unlock watermark-free presentation.

What agencies see

  • • Alphabetical board view
  • • Portfolio detail with stats & bio
  • • Sticky booking CTA
  • • Downloadable assets
